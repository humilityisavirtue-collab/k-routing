## What you can build with this

- **Cost-reduced AI backend** — Drop openclaw in front of any LLM API. 80% of calls vanish.
- **Multi-agent systems** — OpenPod gives your agents a coordinate system instead of hardcoded routing.
- **Auditable AI** — Every query has a visible K-vector. "The model said so" becomes "the query routed through +7S at tier 3."
- **Semantic search** — K-vectors cluster. Similar queries have adjacent addresses.
- **Customer service** — Template-first routing for support bots. Most questions don't need a model.
